About Me

Hi there! I’m Jasmine Albarghsh – I have a rich background as a research specialist, now charting a new course into the dynamic world of data analytics. My background has given me a solid grasp of how data can shape amazing user experiences and drive impactful decisions in research studies. I’ve spent years with hands-on experience in data collection and project management for both UX studies and Clinical Trials, and now I’m eager to dive into analytics to uncover the stories behind the numbers.

When I’m not immersed in data projects, you’ll find me experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, lost in a great book, or exploring new places with friends. My latest adventure was a solo four-month backpacking trip through South America, where I had the chance to hike in Patagonia – definitely the highlight of my year (and yes, that’s my picture above!)

Let’s connect and see how we can turn data into something extraordinary together. I’m excited to bring my passion and fresh perspective to new projects!
